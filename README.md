# OperateDaRocket

In OperateDaRocket you gonna follow this main lore 

**The game is based in 2100s when a top secret space exploration mission called ISTM(Intestellar Space Travel Mission) starts. The registration for the mission is a big complex web of puzzles that takes you to the place where your true training and selection for the ONE AND ONLY crew. You are selected but you were selected due to a system error. The ISEP(the mainstream space agency in this timeline) finds this out but it WAS TOO LATE our protagonist manages to get in the rocket before they take him out and now you are wandering in the space with no hope because you will be executed if you go on earth because right after he goes up, the great war IV begins.**

in this game you gonna solve some puzzles and over
